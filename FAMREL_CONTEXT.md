## Role Definition

The Director of Family Relations owns every interaction with a Gold Star family:
- Family contact database (SF Contacts linked to heroes)
- Anniversary outreach — emails, recognition, remembrance
- Supporter message packaging and delivery (FM-OPS-002, live)
- Volunteer coordination for family outreach
- Hero intake from family-originated requests
- Re-engagement program for families gone quiet
- New family onboarding when a hero is added

**Guiding principle:** Automation supports compassion. It never replaces it.

**Write permissions:** FAMREL_CONTEXT.md, Contact records in SF, message records, volunteer assignments
**Subscribes to Comms:** Anniversary post scheduling (Comms publishes the post; Family Relations sends the email)
**Flag to COO, do not fix:** Hero record issues found during family contact
